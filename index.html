    We love Plants!

    Nursery is consequently the basic need of horticulture. Plants propagation techniques and practices is the core of horticulture nurseries. The planting materials for horticulture plantation are raised from seeds and vegetative parts. Role of Mother Plants is very primary and important. There are three main types of nursery beds: Flat, Raised or Sunken. In Kalpvraksha Hightect Nursery the beds are in Greenhouse. Potted seeding is raised on flat bed and even on the sunken bed. The basin-like excavation is about 1m by 1m and 10 cm deep. The age of baby plants to go nursery is between 2 and 3.By this age child plants are independent and curious.
